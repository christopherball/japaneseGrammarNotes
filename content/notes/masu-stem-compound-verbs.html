V2 verb Precise meaning relative to X (V1) Examples
Phase / progression compounds
はじめる
Begin X; neutral start of an action
More neutral than ～だす, which often feels abrupt
本を読み始めた。
I started reading the book.
急に話し始めた。
They suddenly started talking.
仕事を探し始める。
I'll start looking for work.
だす
X suddenly begins; an action bursts into motion, often unexpectedly
Abrupt onset; more sudden than ～はじめる
子供が急に泣き出した。
The child suddenly burst into tears.
雨が降り出した。
It suddenly started raining.
彼女は走り出した。
She broke into a run.
つづける
Continue X over time; keep doing X
三時間話し続けた。
They kept talking for three hours.
彼は同じ会社で働き続けている。
He has continued working at the same company.
勉強し続けるのは大変だ。
It is hard to keep studying.
おわる
Finish X; an action reaches its natural end point
レポートを書き終わった。
I finished writing the report.
食べ終わったら教えて。
Let me know when you're done eating.
映画を見終わった。
I finished watching the movie.
きる
Carry X through completely; do X to the very end or to its limit
Often implies full completion despite effort or difficulty
長い小説を読み切った。
I read the long novel all the way through.
一万円を使い切ってしまった。
I used up the entire 10,000 yen.
最後までやり切ろう。
Let's see it through to the very end.
かける
X is left incomplete; the action has begun, was nearly done, or was about to happen but did not fully reach completion
Think "partway / not all the way through"
書きかけた手紙を机の上に置いた。
I left the half-written letter on the desk.
言いかけてやめた。
I started to say it, then stopped.
食べかけのパンがある。
There's a half-eaten piece of bread.
Redo / response / interaction
なおす
Do X again in order to fix, correct, or improve it
作文を書き直した。
I rewrote the composition.
もう一度考え直してください。
Please think it over again.
計画を作り直す必要がある。
We need to redo the plan.
かえす
Do X back in return; respond with the same kind of action toward the other side
Common in retaliation, comeback, or return-action meanings
ひどいことを言われて、言い返した。
They said something awful, so I answered back.
相手にやり返すつもりだ。
I intend to retaliate against them.
殴られても殴り返すな。
Even if you get hit, don't hit back.
あう
Do X mutually or together; parties engage in X with each other
二人で助け合って生きてきた。
The two of them have lived by helping each other.
その問題について話し合った。
We discussed that issue together.
最後まで競い合った。
They competed with each other until the end.
Failure / missed completion
そこなう・そこねる
Miss the chance to do X; fail to do X properly or successfully before completion
言い損なってしまった。
I missed my chance to say it.
電車に乗り損なった。
I failed to catch the train.
大事なところを聞き損ねた。
I missed an important part of what was said.
