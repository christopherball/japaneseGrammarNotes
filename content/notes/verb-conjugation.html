Notes

  • The ichidan potential form 食べられる has the same surface form as the passive, so context determines which meaning is intended.
  • The same overlap appears with こられる, which can be either the potential or passive form of くる.
  • Godan verbs that end in う use わ (instead of あ) for negative form (買う → 買わない).
